Special Items
Special Items

What exactly the special items gives to you. Well if you want to heal yourself after escaped fight or after lost fight you can buy food from that shop. If you click on item the power of it will be displayed. Power indicates how many health points it restore.
Usage of these items are really simple just click on it in your inventory and if your health is less than 100 you can eat them.

Arena

To fight in the arena there is only one requirement: Your HP has to be more than zero. There are times when you run out of gold and you don't have enough food to revive yourself so then you can click REVIVE button which is placed under your Character panel.
To fight in the arena simply click the button in the top navigation bar or click the button in your Character panel.
